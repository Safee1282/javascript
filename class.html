Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga is a type of yoga that focuses on physical techniques to improve strength, flexibility, and mental concentration. It also emphasizes breath control, posture, and mindfulness.

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is a type of yoga that is part of Patanjali's Yoga Sutras. It is based on the idea that physical and mental health are best achieved through a combination of physical exercise, meditation, and ethical and moral behavior..
